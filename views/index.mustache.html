Data management made simple

Collaborate with groups and report on results

Group Data includes everything you need to get your collaborative data project up and running. Using familiar spreadsheet formats and an easy-to-use interface, you are minutes away from sharing important data with those who need it.

All you need is an email address to sign up and start creating important data projects. Once logged in, you will have the ability to add other users to your projects as well as generate reports based on collaborative data input.

Group Data is completely free to use. All we require is a valid email address to start collaborating with other users.

Upon your first login, you will be guided step-by-step through the process of adding and editing projects, adding data, adding users, and generating reports.

Easily import existing spreadsheets to projects. Your Group Data projects can also be downloaded for use on the spreadsheet editor of your choice.

Generate interactive pie charts automatically according to the data you and your team mates have entered.
